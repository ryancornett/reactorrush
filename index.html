How to Play

  • Click/tap inside the game area to start.
  • Hold ◀/▶ (or bottom left/right) to tilt the control rod.
  • The neutron (ball) always wants to go up.
  • Collect nuclei & photons for points.
  • Green fuel pellets heal one health block.
  • Build combos to reach CRITICAL (x5)!
  • Press P for the pause menu, M to mute.
